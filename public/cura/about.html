About Cura

Healthcare, Reimagined

We believe care shouldn't stop at the hospital walls. Cura unifies bedside data and remote expertise into a single, secure cloud platform.

Our Mission

Closing the gap between bedside & expertise

Cura was born from a simple observation: critical clinical decisions are too often delayed by paper charts, scattered systems, and the physical distance between patients and specialists. We're here to remove every one of those frictions.

Our Story

From sketch to platform

A short journey of how Cura came to life.

💡
The Spark
Sharon, Aaron and Sam noticed how clinical decisions were slowed down by fragmented tools — a whiteboard sketch became the first wireframe.
🛠️
First Prototype
A vitals form synced to a single doctor dashboard. It was crude, but it proved that real-time clinical data could feel effortless.
🚀
Going Multi-Role
Nurses, doctors, patients, and admins each got dedicated portals — every interface scoped to their workflow and permissions.
🌟
Today
Cura is a complete cloud medical consultation platform — and we're just getting started.
