How We Work

Three builders, one principle

Every line of code, every pixel, every decision is filtered through a single question: does this make care safer or faster?

🎯
Clinician-First
We sit with nurses and doctors before we write a single line. The product follows the workflow, not the other way around.
🔁
Ship & Iterate
Small, safe releases. Real feedback loops. We'd rather get one thing perfect than ten things "fine".
🤝
Radical Honesty
If something doesn't work for the user, we say so — to each other and to our partners. No precious egos.
