Frequently Asked

Got Questions?

Everything you might want to know — about security, roles, pricing, and getting started.

Is patient data secure on Cura? +
Yes — every record is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.3. Role-based access control ensures users only see records they're explicitly authorized for, and every read/write is audit-logged.
Do I need any special hardware? +
No. Cura runs entirely in your browser — laptop, tablet, or phone. The only requirement is a modern browser and an internet connection.
How do the four roles differ? +
Administrators manage users and settings. Nurses log vitals and observations. Doctors view records, write prescriptions, and run consultations. Patients view their own data, prescriptions, and chat with their doctor.
Can patients book their own appointments? +
Yes. Patients can request appointments directly from their dashboard, and doctors see them in a unified calendar with status tracking.
How quickly do vitals reach the doctor? +
Instantly. The moment a nurse hits "Submit", the values appear on the assigned doctor's dashboard with abnormal readings highlighted automatically.
Is there a mobile app? +
Cura is a fully responsive web app — it works beautifully on phones and tablets without needing an install. Native apps are on the roadmap.
How do I get started? +
Click "Get Started" or "Sign In", pick a role, and try the demo with any email and password — you'll be inside the dashboard in seconds.
Who built Cura? +
Cura is crafted by Sharon, Aaron and Sam — a small, focused team obsessed with making healthcare feel modern. Read more on our Team page.
